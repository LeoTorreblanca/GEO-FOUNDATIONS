Purpose

GEO Foundations is the public mathematical core of the GEO framework. It develops a conservative geometric architecture before introducing physical interpretation, observational applications, or implementation-specific studies.

The objective is to provide a coherent and inspectable foundation from which application-oriented GEO projects may be developed and evaluated independently.

Scope

This repository is intentionally limited to mathematical foundations. Physical interpretations, cosmological applications, computational laboratories, and validation workflows are maintained in independent GEO repositories.

The repository should therefore be read as a foundational mathematical construction, not as a claim of experimental confirmation or a replacement for established physical models.

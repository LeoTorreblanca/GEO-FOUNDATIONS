# GEO-GEOMETRY

## Geometric Foundations of the GEO Framework

GEO-GEOMETRY provides the geometric realization of the GEO framework.

Its purpose is to describe how observable space emerges from primitive conservative organization through a sequence of necessary geometric constructions.

Rather than postulating hidden dimensions, isolated objects or predefined metrics, GEO-GEOMETRY develops geometry as an emergent consequence of conservative organization.

Observable space is therefore not primitive.

It is the projected realization of a deeper geometric structure.

---

# Core Principle

Geometry exists before observation.

Primitive organization exists before geometry becomes observable.

Observation does not construct space.

Observation reveals a projected representation of an already organized conservative structure.

The visible universe is therefore the consequence of geometry rather than its origin.

The GEO framework assumes that primitive organization is conserved.

Observable geometry appears only after this internal organization becomes projectively accessible.

The framework therefore proceeds from primitive organization to observable space.
